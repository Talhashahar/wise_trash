The Purpose

Efficient management of trash-cans collection has become a major cause of concern for municipalities in developing countries in the past few years. With the increase in population, the need for improving the common waste collection system has dramatically increased. The traditional operating waste collection services cost a lot to the municipalities, while the scenarios of overflowing trash cans and traffic delays are a major annoyance repeating itself in many of those cities.

The Method

Traditionally, municipalities operate on fixed routes to pick up trash and recyclables on designated days, regardless of whether the trash cans/containers are full or not. [http://www.brokenarrowok.gov/DocumentCenter/View/306/BA-sanitation-analysis?bidId=] This project aims to provide an embedded IOT system to enhance and improve the current waste collection service. WiseTrash will optimize waste collection management and ultimately reduce resources and operating costs. The solution will focus on providing a modular system, such that will provide an architecture and infrastructure enabling a smooth path for improvements and upgrades. Further, the massive data will be widely available to many subscribers for research.
